Here's what you will need:

1. a banana [or half if you are using the bigger varieties]
2. 3 tbsp of maida or wheatflour
3. one egg
4. 2 tbsp of sugar
5. 3 tbsp of milk
6. 3 tbsp of vegetable oil or butter
7. 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
